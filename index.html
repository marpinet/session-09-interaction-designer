About

I'm Mariana, an interactive designer with experience leading creative teams, building cross-disciplinary projects and shaping experiences that connect brands and people. I founded Marabunta Creative Agency, where I serve as Creative Director and help clients translate ideas into immersive digital products and campaigns.

My practice mixes product thinking, visual design and interactive prototyping. I focus on clarity, delight and measurable impact.
